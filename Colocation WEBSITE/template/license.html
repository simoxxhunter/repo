Icons

Icons are sourced from Unicons and Material Icons. You can check out the Unicons license here and Material Icons license here.

To use a Unicon, visit the Unicons library and select the icon you wish to use. Once you have selected the icon, you can find its unicode symbol on the right sidebar next to the icon's name. Simply click and copy the unicode symbol to use the icon.

To use a Material icon, visit the Material Icons library and select the icon you wish to use. Once you have selected the icon, you can find its class on the right sidebar. Simply click and copy the icon class to use the icon.
